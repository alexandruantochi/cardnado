How Does It Work?

Cardnado works by letting users share their loyalty cards anonymously. When you use the app:

  1. You get a random card from our pool of shared cards
  2. The barcode updates periodically to a different card
  3. Your shopping habits remain private while still getting discounts
  4. Adding your own card helps grow the pool for everyone

Note: This is a community-driven project. The more people share their cards, the better it works for everyone!
